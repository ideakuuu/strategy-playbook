# Onboarding Checklist (24-hour SLA)

1. Sales handoff received with filled onboarding form.
2. Create client folder in Notion / Drive.
3. Assign project owner and outsource team leads (content, distribution, analytics) — within 1 hour.
4. Kickoff email to client with meeting link, key contacts, and 72-hour delivery expectations — within 2 hours.
5. Schedule kickoff call (within first 24 hours).
6. Fill initial CST entry: objectives, KPIs, timelines, primary contacts.
7. Request access and assets: logos, socials, website, whitepaper, token contract, CoinMarketCap admin access if applicable.
8. Create and share content calendar draft (first 14 days).
9. First deliverable assignment (e.g., CMC content submission or metadata audit) — within 24 hours.
10. Confirm client training on reporting cadence and Slack/comms channel.
