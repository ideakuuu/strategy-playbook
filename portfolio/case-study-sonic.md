# Case Study: Sonic — Token Visibility & Narrative Lift (Sample Playbook)

**Objective:** Increase Sonic token visibility on CoinMarketCap and drive narrative velocity during a new feature launch.

**Strategy**
- Coordinate a content cadence: 3 explainer articles (founders interview, product deep-dive, integration announcement), 2 native CoinMarketCap content placements, and 4 social snippets for partner channels.
- Optimize metadata for CMC: Ensure token profile, website, whitepaper, and social links are up-to-date; upload creatives and one-line pitch.
- Performance push: Paid content amplifications and influencer micro-campaign to drive traffic to the CMC token page.

**Execution**
- Day 0–1: Onboarding checklist completed; roles assigned to content creator, copy editor, and channel manager.
- Day 1–7: Draft content and submit to Sonic for approvals. Submit CoinMarketCap content placements per CMC guidelines.
- Week 2: Launch content; monitor traffic and social mentions; iterate.

**KPIs & (Sample) Results**
- CoinMarketCap profile views: +220% week-over-week during campaign.
- Organic referral clicks to website from CMC: +85%.
- Earned media mentions: 6 articles across crypto niche outlets.
- Client satisfaction: Net Promoter Score (NPS) simulated: 9/10.

**Retention & Upsell Opportunities**
- Offer monthly narrative maintenance package (quarterly feature push + content refresh).
- Propose A/B tests for headlines and metadata to further lift CTR from CMC.

**Takeaways**
- Rapid onboarding and precise content timelines are essential for leveraging product launches.
- Use CMC as a persistent referral engine; maintain updated metadata and produce repeatable micro-stories.
